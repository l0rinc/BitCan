Basic setup
Durable setup

The average aluminium can may melt in direct fire. To enhance fire resistance, consider:

  1. Placing it in a fireproof container filled with sand or salt, which theoretically could absorb some of the heat.
    Note: This method has not been empirically tested yet.
  2. Stamping the symbols onto stainless steel dog tags that are chained together.
    Note: stainless steel is more challenging to stamp.

    As an added bonus, dog tags resemble a personal keepsake, which may deter accidental disposal while reducing the likelihood of deliberate theft.

Encoding Instructions
Sticker Template

Print the template, stick it on az aluminium can, draw your seed phrase on the print, then stamp through the sticker with a screwdriver.

Details
  1. Securely generate your mnemonic seed phrase using a trusted, offline hardware wallet.
  2. Flatten an aluminium can into a sheet and then attach the printed template to it - or draw it onto a piece of paper and affix it to the can.
  3. Translate the words into glyphs, ensuring each corresponds accurately to the provided list.
  4. Stamp each glyph carefully onto the metal, double-checking for precision and verifying the sequence by reviewing it in reverse.
  5. Use a screwdriver for stamping the glyphs into the metal.
  6. To ensure security and privacy, store the stamped metal in a location that is both secure and discreet.

Decoding Instructions
  1. First, transcribe all glyphs carefully onto a sheet of paper. This will serve as a more reliable reference than working directly from the plate.
  2. Identify the bottom square of each glyph first, as it provides a stable starting point for the decoding process.
  3. Then, identify the top square of the glyph to refine your search within the identified section of the word list.
  4. Alternatively, you can use binary search to navigate through the word list, since each line in the glyph halves the search area.
  5. Verify the transcribed seed phrase with your wallet to ensure its accuracy and integrity.
